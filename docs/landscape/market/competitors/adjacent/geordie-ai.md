## What It Does

Enterprise AI agent governance platform — lifecycle visibility, detection, prevention, and response across all AI agent deployments. Not MCP-specific. Targets the CISO-level buyer who needs a governance layer across the entire organisation's AI agent footprint.

## Coverage

Broad enterprise governance, detection, compliance, lifecycle management.

## Relationship to Airlock

Geordie targets the same enterprise security buyer but at the platform level rather than the MCP content layer. If Airlock lands the enterprise policy management tier (Phase 2), Geordie is the likely comparison. More complementary than competing on the provenance problem specifically.
