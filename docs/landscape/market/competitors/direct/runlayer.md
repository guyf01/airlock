# Runlayer

**Category:** Funded startup — MCP security gateway
**Funding:** $11M seed (November 2025) — Khosla Ventures (Keith Rabois), Felicis Ventures
**Angel:** David Soria Parra, lead creator of the MCP spec
**Customers:** 8 enterprise customers within 4 months of launch — Gusto, Rippling, Instacart, Ramp + 4 others
**Source:** https://techcrunch.com/?p=3068049

## What It Does

MCP security gateway. Threat detection on every MCP request. Observability across MCP servers. Permissions integrated with Okta/Entra. Positions as "all-in-one MCP security."

## Coverage

Access control, threat detection, audit logging.

## What It Does NOT Cover

Content-level trust provenance classification. No Spotlighting. No per-endpoint trust registry. No developer confirmation prompts based on content origin. Their framing is access control + observability.

## Strategic Risk

Runlayer is the most direct competitive threat. They have the MCP spec creator as an advisor, the right investors, and enterprise traction already. Their current product does not address content provenance, but they have every capability to expand in that direction. Watch for product announcements.
